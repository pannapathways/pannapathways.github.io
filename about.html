About Panna Pathways

Who We Are

Panna Pathways was founded in Nashik, Maharashtra, with a simple yet profound belief: true development happens when skill meets mindset. We are a training, development, and consulting organization that works with individuals, corporate teams, and startups to unlock their potential — not just through knowledge, but through emotional intelligence and practical application.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic or professional knowledge and real-world effectiveness. We equip people with the emotional resilience, clarity, and interpersonal skills needed to navigate complexity.

Our Vision

A world where every individual and organization approaches growth holistically — combining competence with compassion, strategy with self-awareness.

Training Philosophy

Skill + Mindset + Emotional Intelligence — our three-legged stool. We believe sustainable growth requires more than just technical know-how. It demands self-awareness, the ability to manage emotions, and a mindset oriented toward learning. That's why every program we design balances practical tools with psychological readiness.

Founder & Visionary

Dr. Snehal A. Bagul

Founder & Lead Consultant

Behind Panna Pathways is a passionate educator and mentor dedicated to shaping careers and unlocking individual potential. With a Ph.D. in Management and a strong foundation in workplace psychology and human behavior, she brings both academic depth and real-world experience to her work.

As a former Training and Placement Officer, she has closely guided students and professionals, helping them navigate career paths with clarity and confidence. She is a Certified Soft Skills Trainer, EQ (Emotional Intelligence) Trainer, and Career Counsellor, with additional expertise in Memory Management and Human Behaviour Counselling.

Her approach focuses on building not just skills, but mindset, confidence, and long-term growth.

"Through Panna Pathways, she is committed to empowering individuals with practical knowledge, industry-relevant skills, and the confidence to succeed in today's evolving professional world."
